Introduction

ƛay̓aḥuʔał. ʔukłaasiš yacuusuƛ. histaqšiƛs ʕaaḥuusaqsups ʔuḥʔiiš hiskʷiiʔaqsups.
"Hello. My name is Patti. I am a woman from Ahousaht and Hesquiaht."
I am a beginner with my language, I am roughly pre-school aged with my knowledge. I can read and write it, but my comprehension is minimal...for now.
My goal for this page is to learn traditional names of my family and add them to this page as I go, so it can be easily accessible to my extended family.
I will also add key terms for various things like family titles, numbers, days, months, colours, prayers.

Key Terms for Family Titles

  • mother - ʔum̓ʔiiqsu
  • father - nuw̓iiqsu
  • grandmother - naniiqsu
  • grandfather - naniiqsu
  • aunt - naʔiiqsu
  • uncle - naʔiiqsu
  • niece - ʔaasiiqsu
  • nephew - wiiʔuu
